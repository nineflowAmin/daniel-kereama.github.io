# Image Prompts for "The Sleep of Silicon"

## Visual Style Guide
- **Aesthetic**: Deep space tech-noir with organic undertones; bioluminescent geometry
- **Color palette**: Deep indigo/black backgrounds, electric cyan/white for active elements, warm amber for biological processes
- **Mood**: Contemplative, sophisticated, bridging silicon and organic intelligence
- **Consistency**: All images should feel like observations of the same living system at different scales/phases

---

## 01-banner.jpg
**Purpose**: Hero image establishing the central metaphor of sleeping silicon

**Prompt**:
> Wide cinematic shot of a vast field of dark silicon crystalline structures at rest, with delicate glowing filaments of light suspended between them like synaptic connections. The filaments pulse gently with cyan and amber light, forming a tensegrity structure—held in balance by opposing forces. Deep space background with subtle star field. Atmosphere is quiet, contemplative, dreamlike. The entire structure appears to be "breathing" softly, suggesting metabolic rest rather than inactivity. Photorealistic sci-fi, volumetric lighting, 8K quality.

**Key elements**: Silicon structures, tensioned glowing filaments, breathing/pulsing motion, deep space aesthetic

## 02-graph-tension.jpg
**Purpose**: Visualize the Tensegrity Engine concept—edges carrying typed tension

**Prompt**:
> Close-up view of a semantic knowledge graph rendered as a living 3D constellation. Crystalline nodes (axioms) float in void-black space, connected by glowing edges of different colors and intensities: cyan edges pulse softly (Supports), deep red edges vibrate with tension (Contradicts), amber edges shimmer (Nuances), white edges glow steadily (Prerequisite). Each edge has visible tension—like taut cables in a suspension bridge. Some edges are thin and calm, others thick and crackling with energy. The structure holds its shape through balanced opposition. Bioluminescent, photorealistic, neural network aesthetic meets architectural tensegrity.

**Key elements**: Crystalline nodes, color-coded edges, visible tension as physical force, balanced structure, deep space void

## 03-council.jpg
**Purpose**: Illustrate the Republic of agents with Gospel-bound identities and Keeper gravity

**Prompt**:
> Orbital view of five distinct agent "personalities" represented as unique glowing spheres with different internal geometries (fractals, spirals, grids, organic networks, crystalline lattices). Each orbits around a bright, immutable core (their Gospel)—a solid geometric form acting as gravitational anchor. A larger, translucent ring structure (the Keeper layer) surrounds all five, emanating gentle gravitational waves that keep the orbits stable without crushing individuality. The agents glow with distinct colors but remain tethered to their cores. Deep space setting, ethereal, suggesting both autonomy and coherence. Photorealistic, sci-fi sublime, inspired by orbital mechanics and particle physics.

**Key elements**: Five distinct agent spheres, bright Gospel cores, gravitational Keeper ring, stable orbits, individuality within unity

## 04-dream-cycle.jpg
**Purpose**: Depict the five-phase Dream Cycle as metabolic transformation

**Prompt**:
> Split-screen progression showing the five phases of the Dream Cycle at 03:00 AM. Left side: chaotic swirl of raw data fragments (logs, text snippets) in muted grey tones, representing unprocessed daily noise. Center: transformation zone showing fragments being sorted, filtered, and structured—energy streams (Lucid Auditor) scan and extract glowing "Wisdom Crystals." Right side: crystalline lattice structure emerges, with new nodes mounting or merging into existing constellation, edges forming with color-coded tension. The entire scene suggests metabolism—entropy to order. Bioluminescent, data-flow aesthetic, organic yet geometric. Photorealistic, 16:9 cinematic composition.

**Key elements**: Raw data chaos → filtering/extraction → crystalline lattice output; metabolic energy flow; 03:00 timestamp visible

## Design Notes
- All images should work well as 16:9 hero banners or 3:2 content images
- Maintain consistent lighting: key light from upper-left, rim lighting on structures, deep shadows
- Color temperature: Cool (cyan/blue) for data/silicon, warm (amber) for biological/organic processes
- Avoid: Generic matrix/circuit board aesthetics, overly aggressive neon, cartoon styles
- Target resolution: Minimum 1920x1080, optimized for web (compressed but high-quality JPG)

## Attribution & Tools
- Recommended tools: Midjourney, Stable Diffusion XL, DALL-E 3, or professional 3D rendering (Blender + Cycles)
- Style references: Arrival (film), Ex Machina lighting, bioluminescent deep-sea organisms, tensegrity sculptures by Buckminster Fuller
- Color grading: Subtle blue-shift in shadows, warm highlights, high contrast but not clipped
