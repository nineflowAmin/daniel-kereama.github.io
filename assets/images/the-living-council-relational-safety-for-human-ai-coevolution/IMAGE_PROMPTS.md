# Image Prompts for "The Living Council" Article

## Universal Style Guidelines

**Natural System Metaphors:** Use organic forms from nature—rivers, trees, ecosystems, weather patterns, growth rings, mycelial networks, coral reefs, forest canopies, root systems, and symbiotic relationships.

**Subconscious Symbols:** Integrate universal patterns—spirals (growth, evolution), circles (wholeness, cycles), fractals (self-similarity at scale), mandalas (integration), and the golden ratio (harmony).

**Color Philosophy:** 
- **Deep blues/purples** (depth, wisdom, structure)
- **Warm golds/ambers** (wisdom, memory, warmth)
- **Forest greens** (growth, life, relationship)
- **Earthy browns** (foundation, stability)
- **Soft whites/pearls** (clarity, transparency)
- **Strategic contrast** (light/shadow for depth)

**Visual Language:** Blend organic flow with geometric precision—like a tree's growth (organic) following mathematical patterns (geometric). Show both structure and fluidity coexisting.

**Emotional Resonance:** Each image should evoke a feeling before it conveys information—trust, growth, harmony, resilience, or transformation.

## Enhanced Style Guidelines

**Natural System Integration:**
- Always use organic forms from nature as the primary visual language
- Integrate universal patterns (spirals, circles, fractals, mandalas) that trigger subconscious recognition
- Show growth, evolution, and transformation as natural processes

**Color Psychology:**
- **Deep blues/purples:** Wisdom, depth, structure, the unknown
- **Warm golds/ambers:** Memory, wisdom, warmth, the known
- **Forest greens:** Growth, life, relationship, health
- **Earthy browns:** Foundation, stability, the ground of being
- **Soft whites/pearls:** Clarity, transparency, purity of intention
- **Strategic contrast:** Light/shadow for depth, showing both sides

**Visual Metaphors:**
- Trees = structure, growth, wisdom
- Rivers = flow, adaptation, finding the way
- Spirals = evolution, growth, recursion
- Circles = wholeness, cycles, completion
- Networks = connection, relationship, interdependence
- Light/shadow = transparency, honesty, depth

**Emotional Resonance:**
Each image should evoke a feeling before conveying information. The viewer should feel trust, growth, harmony, resilience, or transformation before they understand the concept intellectually.

**Technical Integration:**
Mathematical patterns (Fibonacci, golden ratio, fractals) should be present but feel natural, not imposed. Like how a tree's growth follows mathematical principles but feels completely organic.
