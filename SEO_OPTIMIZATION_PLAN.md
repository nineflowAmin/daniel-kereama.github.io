## 3. On-Page SEO Elements

### A. Post Front Matter (Required Fields)
```yaml
---
layout: post
title: "Optimized Title with Primary Keyword"
date: YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS +0000
categories: [category1, category2]
description: "Compelling 150-160 character meta description with keywords"
excerpt: "Longer excerpt for preview (2-3 sentences)"
post_slug: "url-friendly-slug"
image: "/assets/images/post-slug/banner.jpg" # For OG image
author: "Daniel Kereama"
tags: [tag1, tag2, tag3] # Additional tags beyond categories
---
```

### B. Content Structure
